View Project

Cifar10 Classifier less than 10 layers

Cifar10 data set is consisted of 32x32 50K training images and 10K testing images. The restriction is that the deep learning model should be less than equal to 10 layers. For making the best performance within 10 layers, tuning hyperparameters and input augmentation is applied. I also implemented ResNet of 10 layers but the performance is lower than 9 layer CNN because of the characteristic of ResNet.
Technical stack: Pytorch, Google Collaboratory, CuDNN

View Project

Object Detector by Background Subtraction

With a given video, the object detector is implemented by the background subtraction algorithm. Weighted sum of the images creates the background image and the each video frame is subtracted from the background image. The performance is calculated based on sensitivity and specificity.
Technical stack: OpenCV-python

MNIST classifier with only 10K training data

MNIST is the basic dataset for ML beginner. It is consisted of 55K training data, 10K testing data, and 5K validation data. In this project, only 10K training data are used.
